
# How to Buy NUERALLY Tokens with USDT

This guide specifically details the process for purchasing NUERALLY tokens using **USDT (BEP-20)** on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network via MetaMask or Trust Wallet.

### üìã Prerequisites (Before You Start)

Ensure your wallet meets the following two requirements. If you do not have both, the transaction will not work.

1.  **USDT Balance:** You must have the amount of USDT you wish to invest (Minimum **10 USDT**).
2.  **BNB Balance (Gas Fees):** You MUST have at least **$1.00 - $2.00 worth of BNB** (approx. 0.003 BNB) in your wallet to pay for network transaction fees.
    *   *Why?* Every action on the blockchain (Approving and Buying) costs a small fee paid in BNB. You cannot pay this fee with USDT.

---

### üöÄ Step-by-Step Purchase Guide

#### 1. Access the Presale
1.  Open your wallet app (MetaMask or Trust Wallet).
2.  Use the **internal browser** inside the app. Do not use Safari or Chrome.
3.  Navigate to: `https://nuerally.io` (or your specific presale URL).

#### 2. Connect Wallet
1.  Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button on the presale interface.
2.  Select your wallet type (e.g., MetaMask).
3.  Approve the connection request in your wallet.
4.  Ensure your wallet is connected to the **BNB Smart Chain (BSC) Mainnet**.

#### 3. Enter Amount
1.  Locate the "You Pay (USDT)" input box.
2.  Enter the amount of USDT you wish to invest (e.g., `100`).
    *   *Note: The minimum purchase is 10 USDT.*
3.  The system will automatically calculate how many NUERALLY tokens you will receive.

#### 4. Approve USDT (Transaction 1 of 2)
1.  Click the button labeled **"Approve USDT"**.
2.  Your wallet will prompt you to set a "spending cap" or approve the contract.
3.  **Confirm** this transaction in your wallet.
    *   *Cost: A few cents in BNB.*
4.  Wait a few seconds for the button to change.

#### 5. Complete Purchase (Transaction 2 of 2)
1.  Once approved, the button will change to **"Buy Now (USDT)"** or **"Buy with USDT"**.
2.  Click this button.
3.  Your wallet will prompt you to confirm the actual purchase transaction.
4.  **Confirm** this transaction.
    *   *Cost: A few cents in BNB.*

#### 6. Confirmation
1.  Wait for the "Purchase Successful!" alert on the website.
2.  Your NUERALLY tokens are now allocated to your wallet address within the presale contract.

### ‚ùì Troubleshooting

*   **Button stuck on "Processing..."**: check your wallet's "Activity" tab. You may have a pending transaction that needs confirmation.
*   **"Insufficient Funds" Error**: This almost always means you have **0 BNB** or not enough BNB for gas fees. Deposit $2 worth of BNB and try again.
*   **Mobile Issues**: Ensure you are using the browser **built into** MetaMask/Trust Wallet, not your phone's default browser.
