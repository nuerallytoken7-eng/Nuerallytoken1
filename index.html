What is NUERALLY?

AI-assisted capital strategies. Treasury discipline. On-chain distribution.
Utility Protocol

NUERALLY is an AI-assisted revenue protocol designed to remove emotional decision-making from capital deployment. The protocol executes automated strategies, aggregates performance at the treasury level, and distributes net protocol revenue to token stakers.

NUERALLY does not promise returns. It engineers systems designed to perform under real market conditions.

How it works

Three-layer architecture designed for disciplined execution.
1) AI Strategy Engine

Algorithms identify volatility and liquidity inefficiencies and deploy predefined strategies under enforced risk parameters.

2) Treasury Management

Capital is managed at the protocol level with exposure caps, performance tracking, and disciplined allocation.

3) On-Chain Distribution

Net protocol revenue is distributed monthly to stakers, with allocations to buyback & burn and treasury growth.

Revenue Engine (MVP)

Launch focus includes volatility capture, market-neutral positioning, and liquidity-driven capital rotation. Revenue is performance-dependent and varies with market conditions.

What NUERALLY avoids

Excessive leverage dependency, single-strategy exposure, and manual trading intervention.

Monthly revenue distribution

Performance-based. Snapshot-driven. Executed on-chain.
  1. Net protocol revenue is calculated
  2. Allocation split between:
    • Token stakers

    • Treasury growth
  3. Snapshot taken
  4. Distribution executed on-chain
