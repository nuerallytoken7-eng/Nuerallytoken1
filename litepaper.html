NUERALLY Litepaper

The Backbone of the AI Agent Economy

1. Executive Summary

NUERALLY is a decentralized utility protocol designed to bridge the gap between high-performance compute demand and capital efficiency. As the AI agent economy explodes, the demand for GPU compute (specifically H100 clusters) has outpaced supply. NUERALLY solves this by creating a decentralized marketplace where users can stake capital to fund GPU rentals for AI agents, earning "Real Yield" in USDT from the revenue generated.

Unlike traditional meme tokens or speculative assets, NUERALLY is built on a revenue-first model. The protocol orchestrates the rental of high-end compute infrastructure, powers autonomous AI agents, and distributes the net profits directly to token stakers in stablecoin (USDT).

2. Market Opportunity: The AI Compute Crunch

The rapid rise of Large Language Models (LLMs) and autonomous agents has created an unprecedented shortage of GPU compute.

  • Surging Demand: Startups and enterprises are competing for limited NVIDIA H100 inventory.
  • Idle Capital: Crypto capital often sits idle or chases inflationary yields.
  • The Gap: There is no efficient bridge connecting decentralized capital with real-world compute infrastructure revenue.

NUERALLY positions itself as this bridge, treating GPU compute as a real-world asset (RWA) that generates verifiable cash flow.

3. The Solution: NUERALLY Protocol

The NUERALLY ecosystem is built on three core pillars designed for disciplined execution:

A. AI Strategy Engine

Automated algorithms identify high-yield opportunities in the compute market. The engine manages the leasing and allocation of GPU clusters to vetted AI agents, optimizing for maximum revenue generation per compute unit.

B. Treasury Management

Capital is managed at the protocol level with strict risk parameters. The treasury ensures that funds are efficiently deployed to lease hardware, while maintaining a buffer for operational sustainability.

C. On-Chain Distribution (Real Yield)

Net protocol revenue is distributed monthly to stakers. Unlike inflationary staking models that pay in native tokens, NUERALLY pays rewards in USDT, ensuring that stakers receive realized value, not diluted paper gains.

NUERALLY is a utility protocol, not a financial security.

  • No Guarantees: Revenue is performance-dependent and varies with market conditions for compute.
  • Technical Risk: Smart contracts, while audited, carry inherent risks.
